My Hobbies & Passions

This page explores three of my favorite interests: hiking/camping, off-roading, and watching movies. Jump to video

  1. Hiking & Camping
    • I have always loved to hike, camp, and backpack! I love spending time in nature and in the mountains every chance I get. Some of my favorite places to do this are in the Unitas, Southern Utah, and the Tetons.
    • I love to plan camping trips with friends and family whenever I get the chance!
  2. Off-Roading
    • My father instilled in me a love for old cars and trucks and taking them off-roading. I enjoy exploring the many trails that all of our beautiful state has to offer.
    • I love to drive and work on my Dad's 1984 Toyota Land Cruiser. She's a looker!
  3. Movie Watching
    • I love wathcing classic films and modern movies! I am a huge fan of Audrey Hepburn and all of her films. I also greatly enjoy the Lord of the Rings, Star Wars, war movies, and more!
    • I try to have a weekly movie night with friends and/or family!
